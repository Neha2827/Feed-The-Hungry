Latest Campaigns

These are the fews on-going Campaigns:

Food Management at individual level

Use Up The Leftovers, Learn The Label Lingo,Be Quirky With Fruits And Vegetables,Check The Refrigerator's Temperature, Shop With A List And Stick To It, Proper Storage,Practice FIFO.

Food Wastage in Social Gathering

Menu Selection(it is better to be aware of what to order and how much to order.),Request for RSVP(RSVP for a good estimation of the quantity of food required),Caterer Selection(make sure that they have a good amount of experience in catering to reduce the chances of wasting food.)

Tieing up with NGOs

we plan to work with various NGOs that are working to control food wastage by distributing excess food in areas where it is needed. These NGOs can be contacted easily before or after your event. People from these NGOs come and take the leftovers and distribute them carefully amongst the people who need food the most.
